Pantry Pal

Ever find yourself unsure of what you can make with that half full bottle of teriyaki sauce? Unused food and leftovers are a problem everyone faces. $165 billion worth of food is wasted each year by Americans. Pantry Pal solves this problem by searching a recipe database and determining which recipes are readily available to make. Users can customize their list of current ingredients and find new or favorite recipes to make. Sign up and start cooking today!

    • Adding Ingredients
    • - Users can quickly and easily add any ingredient in their fridge or pantry
    • - Recipe search results are dynamically filtered for updated recipes in real time
    • - Recipes are sorted by how many missing ingredients the user has
    • Selecting Recipes
    • - Each recipe contains an ingredient list, with pictures, and step-by-step directions
    • - Recipe directions are easy to follow for cooks of all levels
    • - Recipes are drawn from a collection of community-inspired sources
    • Additional Information
    • - Each recipe has nutritional information with common dietary restrictions and a caloric breakdown
    • - Vegetarian, Vegan and other dietary friendly options are clearly shown and highlighted
    • - Percent Daily Values for various vitamin/minerals found in the recipe are also included

Pantry Pal uses the MEAN Stack to handle requests from the client to the server.
